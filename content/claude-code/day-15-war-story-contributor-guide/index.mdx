# Day 15: Writing a war story: the contributor guide

Cables are valuable because they capture real outcomes, not abstract advice.

## What we tried

We standardized every war story to include:

- context under real constraints
- one failed attempt
- one specific adjustment that changed outcome
- measurable impact or clear qualitative result

## What happened

Contributions became more practical, easier to review, and easier for other teams to reuse.

## What we learned

- Lead with a concrete moment, not definitions.
- Keep claims tied to evidence or reproducible behavior.
- Prefer honest tradeoffs over polished narratives.
